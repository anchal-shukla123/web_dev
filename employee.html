Employment Rights

Complete guide to labour laws, job schemes, and grievance redressal platforms.

Understanding Employment Rights

Every working individual deserves fair wages, safe workplaces, and protection against discrimination. Employment rights empower workers to demand dignity and equality at work.

Key Employment Rights in India

  • Minimum Wages Act, 1948: Guarantees fair wages for workers across sectors.
  • Equal Remuneration Act, 1976: Equal pay for equal work without gender bias.
  • Factories Act, 1948: Ensures health, safety, and welfare of industrial workers.
  • Employee Provident Fund (EPF): Mandatory retirement savings for salaried employees.
  • Right Against Unfair Dismissal: Legal protections under Industrial Disputes Act, 1947.

Major Government Schemes for Employment

  • MGNREGA: 100 days guaranteed wage employment for rural households.
  • National Career Service (NCS): Online portal connecting job seekers with employers (Visit NCS).
  • PMKVY (Skill India Mission): Free skill development training for youth.
  • Atmanirbhar Bharat Rozgar Yojana: EPF benefits for new employment generation post-COVID.

Helplines & Support Platforms

  • Labour Helpline (Ministry of Labour & Employment): 155368
  • EPFO Helpdesk: 1800-118-005
  • eShram Portal: Database for unorganized workers (Visit eShram).

Important Court Judgments

  • Bandhua Mukti Morcha vs. Union of India (1984): Right against bonded labour.
  • Vishaka Guidelines (1997): Protection against workplace harassment (especially women).
