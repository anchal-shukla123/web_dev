Women Safety

Complete guide to rights, protections, and support systems for women.

Understanding Women Safety

Women's safety includes protection against violence, harassment, and discrimination. It ensures women enjoy equal rights in public, professional, and private spaces. Globally, an estimated 1 in 3 women experience physical or sexual violence in their lifetime (WHO Report, 2021).

Key Legal Rights for Women in India

  • Zero FIR: File an FIR at any police station, regardless of jurisdiction (CrPC Section 154).
  • Right to Confidentiality: Protects identity of victims of sexual assault (IPC Section 228A).
  • Right Against Workplace Harassment: Protection under POSH Act, 2013.
  • Right to Free Legal Aid: Every woman has access to government-provided legal aid services.
  • Nighttime Protection: Women cannot be arrested after sunset without magistrate's permission (CrPC Section 46).

Major Government Schemes for Women Safety

  • Nirbhaya Fund: Special fund to finance initiatives aimed at women's safety in public spaces.
  • One Stop Centres (OSC): Support services (police, legal, medical, psychological) under one roof for women facing violence.
  • Mahila Shakti Kendras: Empower women through community participation and access to welfare services.
  • Universal Women Helpline - 181: 24/7 emergency response and support system across India.
  • SHE-Box: Online complaint management system against sexual harassment at workplace (Visit SHE-Box).

Helpline Numbers & Resources

  • Police Emergency: 112
  • Women Helpline (All India): 181
  • National Commission for Women: ncw.nic.in
  • Child and Women Protection Units: Available at district police headquarters.

Important Court Judgments

  • Vishaka vs. State of Rajasthan (1997): Laid down guidelines for protection against sexual harassment at the workplace (basis for POSH Act).
  • Lalita Kumari vs. Govt of UP (2014): Mandatory FIR registration for cognizable offences without preliminary inquiry.
  • Independent Thought vs. Union of India (2017): Criminalized marital rape of minor wives below 18 years.
